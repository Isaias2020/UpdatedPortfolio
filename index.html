About Me

Hello, my name is Isaias Quintanilla and welcome to my homepage! I was born in Dallas, Texas and currently live in Fort Worth. Both of my parents were immigrants from a small country in Central America, El Salvador, and migrated to the United States to have a better future for themselves, my sister and I.

I graduated from the University of Texas at Arlington with a Bachelor's Degree in Criminology and Criminal Justice in 2015 with the intention of going to Law School after graduation. I started to work for a law firm in Arlington, Texas since graduation and have been working there since. After working in a law firm environment, I've realized that law school would not be a good fit for me. Since then I have been wanting to go back to school to pursue another career and decided to learn more about Web Development.
